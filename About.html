About LittleMrGoat

Goals

LittleMrGoat strives to provide quality skateboarding videos that showcase the joy of skateboarding, regardless of talent.

History

After a slew of injuries including a broken tailbone, Linus Gordon decided to stay in the world of skateboarding by shooting and editing videos, without the risk of more injuries. Using his dad's camcorder and iMovie, he made his first video, Bangin!: Avi Malina and LittleMrGoat was born. Eventually, Linus bought a new camera, and learned Final Cut in order to create better content for LittleMrGoat's growing popularity.

Fun Facts

The name LittleMrGoat comes from Linus Gordon's initials, whose middle name begins with an 'M'
LittleMrGoat has over 1,000 subscribers and 2,000,000 views
